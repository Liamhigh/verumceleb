Institutional Program

Verum Omnis provides a court-validated investigation and legal automation layer designed to work with your existing in-house counsel and fraud teams. Citizens use the platform for free. For institutions, we offer two subscriptions — Investigation AI and Legal AI — plus an optional success fee of 20% of post-judgment or settled fraud recoveries.

Investigation AI (Subscription)

  • Contradiction Engine & anomaly detection across statements and artefacts.
  • Forensic hashes (SHA-512), QR audit trails, PDF/A-3B evidence packages.
  • Bulk intake pipelines for case queues; triage and prioritisation.
  • Outputs structured for counsel, investigators, and law enforcement.

Legal AI (Subscription)

  • Jurisdiction-aware drafting for notices, affidavits, and filings.
  • Automated bundle assembly with sealed exhibits and chain-of-custody.
  • In-house lawyer workflow: review, approve, e-sign, file.
  • Audit-ready logs for court acceptance and internal policy.

Recovery Model

For institutions, Verum Omnis may participate via a 20% success fee on recovered fraud amounts, payable only after judgment or settlement. This aligns incentives while keeping subscriptions predictable. Citizens pay zero; access to justice remains free for private individuals.

  • Success fee applies to recoveries proven through court orders, settlement agreements, or equivalent instruments.
  • Subscriptions cover platform usage, evidence generation, and counsel-ready outputs.
  • Your in-house counsel directs strategy; Verum Omnis provides automation, evidence integrity and drafting.

Data Handling

Hash-based verification, least-privilege access, immutable logs, exportable evidence packs.

Court Acceptance

Sealed documents with QR & SHA-512 on cover pages; audit trails for admissibility.

Integration

API & bulk intake; maps to your matter IDs, custody policies, and retention schedules.
